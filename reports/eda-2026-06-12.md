## Correlation findings

- **ELO vs win rate validation**: Point-biserial correlation between ELO difference and home win result on 2010+ sample (n=1,242 after ELO join) — ELO diff strongly monotonically predicts win rate. Confirms ELO as the primary team-strength feature.
- **ELO ↔ FIFA rankings**: Both capture similar team-strength orderings; ELO is more predictive (research consensus), FIFA rankings secondary/auxiliary.
- **Transfermarkt top-18 market value**: Broadly correlated with ELO ranking (England, France, Brazil top both) but diverges for some nations (Colombia, Ecuador — strong ELO, lower market value due to player market gaps). Use both as complementary features.

## Red flags for /ds-data-quality

- **Team name harmonization required**: 9 WC 2026 teams missing from `eloratings.csv` by exact name match (South Africa, Ivory Coast, Cape Verde, Bosnia & Herzegovina, DR Congo, United States, New Zealand, Saudi Arabia, South Korea) — likely name variants; need lookup table before joining ELO to fixtures
- **Transfermarkt name mismatches**: Ivory Coast and South Korea missing (confirmed to be naming variants: "Côte d'Ivoire" / "Republic of Korea")
- **ELO freshness**: `eloratings.csv` is 181 days stale (last: 2025-12-13) — pre-tournament WC snapshot `elo_ratings_wc2026.csv` covers all 48 teams and is the correct source for match features
- **FIFA rankings stale**: Last snapshot June 2024 (722 days ago) — use for historical training features only, not as current team strength
- **SPI discontinued**: FiveThirtyEight SPI ends 2023-06-04 — useful only as historical calibration reference
- **Transfermarkt valuations**: ~~stale~~ — **updated** to February 2026 (dcaribou/transfermarkt-datasets, CC0, weekly automated); 14,871 records from 2025+
- **Transfermarkt 34% missing market values**: Need to verify WC squad players (likely top-tier, hence covered); validate coverage for smaller nations
- **PataterieData overlap**: 13,821 matches in PataterieData not in Mart Jürisoo — useful supplement; coverage and quality of extra matches should be verified before merging
- **70 rows with missing scores** in results.csv (0.14%) — historic matches, safely droppable for training
